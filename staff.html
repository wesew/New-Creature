Bud Lambert

Counselor, Clinical Director

Bud Lambert serves part time as a counselor at Walnut Street. Bud has a Masters in Community Counseling from Mississippi College and a Masters in Marriage and Family Therapy from the University of Southern Mississippi. Bud and his wife, Cynthia, live in Alexandria and have three grown children, William, Susanah and Nathan.

Kaitlin Harris

Counseling Intern

Kaitlin Harris and her husband, Wesley Harris, have one son, Charlie. She graduated from Middle Tennessee State University with a Bachelor’s in Special Education in 2011, and she is in her final year at The University of Tennessee at Martin where she is pursuing a Master’s in Clinical Mental Health Counseling. After graduating from MTSU, Kaitlin worked in the Special Education field for three years before coming home to raise her son and complete her Master’s degree. Having experienced the Spiritual Soul Therapy first-hand, Kaitlin is passionate about helping others experience God’s love and come to know Him as a loving Father. After graduating from the Spiritual Leadership Institute, she plans to work as a ministerial counselor and to continue to serve her family.
