About New Creature Counseling

The New Creature Counseling Clinic provides ministerial counseling services to members of the community in Middle Tennessee. All counselors at the center hold a minimum of a Master's Degree in the mental health field and are trained in Spiritual Soul Therapy. Spiritual Soul Therapy is a biblical approach to counseling that uses elements of cognitive behavioral, person-centered, and systems frameworks, but it relies on the unmatched power of the Holy Spirit to create real change in individuals. When a client meets with a Spiritual Soul Therapist, they will be meeting with someone who both understands the mental health aspects of client issues and is committed to helping others experience God's love in a powerful way. New Creature therapists empower clients to seek real change in their attitudes, behaviors, feelings, and interactions within relationships.
